Esther Lugoe Mengi

Esther Mengi is a Partner at CIC from Tanzania and lead consultant in Cyber security and Digital Forensics having extensive knowledge in system programming, system analysis, Security vulnerability assessments and penetration testing on Clients’ Business Systems, Data and networks.

Moreover, she has been guest speaker on Annual ICT professional conference organized by ministry of work, transport and communication – October 2017 (Title: Impact of Cybercrime to Digital economy), 4th Annual ISACA Audit, Risk and Cybersecurity conference - May 2018 (title: Webserver Security), Sahara Sparks 2018 + AfriLabs Annual Gathering 2018 on 11th-13th Oct, 2018 (Title: Women in Technology)

Esther holds Bachelor of Science in Information Systems and Technology in Forensics and Cyber Security from United States International University–Africa (USIU) in addition to certificification on CEH, CCNA, ISO/IEC 27001:2005 and CISA.

She has also been decorated and appeared in Forbes Magazine as among thirty (30) most young promising entrepreneurs in 2017 as well as Gold medalist in 2012 games besides being a member and committee to ISACA Tanzanian Chapter.

James Kamau

James Kamau a director at CIC and lead consultant Forensic Audit having a Certified Fraud Examiner (CFE) and member of the Association of Certified Fraud Examiners (ACFE-Kenyan chapter), he holds a Bachelor of Commerce degree (Finance option)-JKUAT and currently pursuing Master’s degree in Business Administration (Methodist University)

He brings to the firm a wide range of experience gained in various organizations including Family Bank, KEEF Kenya and Palm House Dairies having extensive knowledge in auditing, fraud risk management, and anti-money laundering.

He has conducted several fraud investigations both for local and international organizations including financial institutions, non-governmental organizations, corporate and government related organizations.

In addition, he functions as our expert witness in all cases requiring court attendance. His functionality compliments both the external and internal audit functions of the firm.
