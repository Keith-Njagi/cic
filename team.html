James Kamau, CFE

James Kamau is the Lead consultant, Forensic Audit. He is a Certified Fraud Examiner (CFE) and member of the Association of Certified Fraud Examiners (ACFE-Kenya chapter). James holds a Bachelor of Commerce degree (Finance) and currently pursuing Master’s degree in Business Administration. He has wide spectrum of experience working with different firms in internal and external audit as well as conducting fraud investigations for local and international organizations in various sectors such as financial institutions, non-governmental organizations, governments and corporates.

Michael Yator, CPA

Michael Yator is the Lead Finance and Tax consultant. He is a practicing accountant with long time consulting and corporate experience in Strategic finance management, auditing, accounting and supervision, having built an illustrious career in private sector, SACCO, NGO, county governments and parastatals, serving in management position for over 5-years. He is a member of the Institute of certified Public Accountant (CPA-K) in Kenya. Michael holds Bachelor of Commerce degree in finance.

Michael has been instrumental in streamlining Financial Operations, Systems and Recordkeeping for compliance, formulation of Standard Operating Procedures, Financial system audits, checks and internal controls, Formulation, validation and review of Strategic Plans, developed an operational manual for audit guidelines and checklists for use, developing wide range of business development strategies and financial management objectives for our clients. In addition, Michael has proficiency in forensic and investigative audit work as well as wide exposure to International Public sector Accounting Standards (IPSAS) used in Central Government and Local Authorities and NGO coordination act.

Kevin Mwangi K., CEH

Kevin Mwangi is the Senior consultant in Cyber Security and Digital Forensics at CIC with over 7 years experience in computing having extensive knowledge in software & mobile development, networking, system security, penetration testing, digital forensics and systems audits. He is a member of the Association of Certified Fraud Examiners (ACFE-Kenya chapter) and EC-Council. He has an illustrious career growth working for private sector, non-governmental organization and consultancy services. Kevin holds Bachelor of Science in Information Systems and Technology in IT security and currently pursuing MSc Information Systems (Internet Security and Cybercrime) in addition to certifications in Certified Ethical Hacker (CEH) and CCNA.

Esther Lugoe M., CEH, CISA

Esther has extensive knowledge in system programming, system analysis, Security vulnerability assessments and penetration testing on Clients’ Business Systems, Data and networks.Esther holds Bachelor of Science in Information Systems and Technology in Forensics and Cyber Security from United States International University–Africa (USIU) in addition to certifications in CEH, CCNA, ISO/IEC 27001:2005 and CISA.
